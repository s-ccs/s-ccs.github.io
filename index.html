Stuttgart Computational Cognitive Science

Modelling human activity

The goal of the Stuttgart Computational Cognitive Science group, is to understand human behavior and brain activity under realistic conditions. We focus on advancing methodological approaches in combined EEG (a method to measure brain potentials), Eye-Tracking and Cognitive Modelling. We provide open source tools (e.g. Unfold, Unfold.jl, eegvis and others).

Versatility

We are starting out to combine EEG, Eye-Tracking and VR - stay tuned for more!

Collaboration, not Competition

If you are interested in our work, please reach out! We are committed to collaborate but not to compete!
